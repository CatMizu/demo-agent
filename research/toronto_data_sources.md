# Toronto Municipal and Housing Data Sources

## Key Toronto Data Resources Found

### 1. Toronto Housing Data Hub
**URL**: https://www.toronto.ca/city-government/data-research-maps/toronto-housing-data-hub/

**Key Components**:
- **Toronto Housing Data Book**: Comprehensive insights into Toronto's housing system health and demographic impacts
- **Housing Data**: Information on affordable rental housing and rental replacement housing
- **Affordable Rental Housing Map**: Interactive map of upcoming/completed affordable developments
- **HousingTO 2020-2030 Action Plan Dashboard**: Progress tracking toward housing targets
- **Housing Data Hub Definitions**: Data terminology and definitions

### 2. City of Toronto Open Data Portal
**URL**: https://open.toronto.ca/

**Features**:
- Comprehensive open datasets across all city departments
- Neighbourhood Profiles (2021 update)
- Interactive data exploration tools
- Open data request system for new datasets
- Self-guided data discovery tools

### 3. CMHC Toronto-Specific Data
**URL**: https://www03.cmhc-schl.gc.ca/hmip-pimh/en/TableMapChart/Table?TableId=1.1.4.6&GeographyId=2270&GeographyTypeId=3

**Data Available**:
- Housing market statistics
- Rental market data
- Mortgage and debt information
- Household characteristics
- New construction data
- Home prices and sales

## Key Housing Crisis Indicators Identified

### Current Challenges
- Lack of affordable and adequate housing options
- Rising rental and homeownership costs
- Inflation outpacing income growth
- Disproportionate impact on low-and-moderate income households
- Particular challenges for renters and equity-deserving groups

### Housing Targets
- **Overall Target**: 285,000 housing units
- **Rent-Controlled Homes**: 65,000 units to be approved
- **Affordable Units**: 41,000 units
- **Rent-Geared-to-Income (RGI)**: 6,500 units

## Data Coverage Areas
- Social and affordable housing portfolio
- Rental housing demolition and conversion applications
- Non-profit and private development approvals
- Demographic and income patterns
- Urban performance metrics
