# CMHC Housing Data - Waterloo Region (Kitchener-Cambridge-Waterloo)

## Data Collection Date
January 2025

## Data Sources
- Canada Mortgage and Housing Corporation (CMHC) Housing Market Information Portal
- Cornerstone Association of REALTORS® (MLS data)
- WOWA.ca Housing Market Analysis

## Key Findings

### Home Prices (2024)
- **Average sale price (2024)**: $784,343 (year-to-date)
- **Price change from 2023**: -0.3% decrease
- **Sales volume**: 6,777 homes sold through MLS in 2024 (+2.8% from 2023)
- **Performance vs. 5-year average**: -18.9% decline in sales volume

### Regional Price Variations (Current/Recent Data)
- **Cambridge**: $823,700 average
- **Kitchener**: $817,200 average  
- **Waterloo**: $785,500 average

### MLS Home Price Index (HPI)
- **Monthly change**: +1.0% month-over-month
- **Annual change**: -9.3% year-over-year decline
- **Cambridge HPI**: $676,300 benchmark price (-8.7% year-over-year)
- **Kitchener-Waterloo HPI**: $646,200 benchmark price (-9.3% year-over-year)

### Rental Market Data
- **Purpose-built rental vacancy rate**: Approximately 2.2% (2024)
- **Condominium rental vacancy rate**: 0.4% (significantly lower than purpose-built)
- **Market dynamics**: Condominium apartment owners prioritize quicker rental turnover, contributing to lower vacancy rates

### Market Context
- National rental vacancy rate increased to 2.2% in 2024
- Waterloo Region vacancy rates remain below the 3% threshold typically considered healthy
- Significant difference between purpose-built and condominium rental markets

## Data Quality and Limitations
- MLS data represents reported sales through organized real estate system
- HPI provides more accurate price trends than simple averages by tracking "benchmark" properties
- Rental data may not capture all rental units (e.g., basement apartments, private rentals)
- Some data points are estimates or interpolated from broader regional data
